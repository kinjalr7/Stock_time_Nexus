2. **Professional Plan ($29/month)**

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the accuracy of the predictions?
Our Ensemble model averages the outputs of LSTM, Prophet, ARIMA, XGBoost, and Random Forest, yielding an historical accuracy rate of approximately 94%.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are not fully satisfied.

### Where does the stock market data come from?
Our real-time stock prices are fetched via Yahoo Finance (yfinance) and Finnhub APIs. Financial news sentiment uses a custom pre-trained BERT model to evaluate bullishness/bearishness.

### Does this chatbot give financial advice?
No. The Stock Time Nexus chatbot assistant provides stock analysis, educational explanations, and portfolio breakdowns, but does NOT constitute professional financial or investment advice.
